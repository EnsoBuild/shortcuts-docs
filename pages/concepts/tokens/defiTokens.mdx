# DeFi Tokens

## Summary

DeFi tokens are tokens that represent a DeFi position. DeFi tokens are obtained by depositing underlying tokens, or other DeFi tokens inside of [DeFi protocols](../protocols). 

For example, depositing [WETH](https://etherscan.io/token/0xc02aaa39b223fe8d0a0e5c4f27ead9083c756cc2) into [Yearn](https://yearn.finance/) you will obtain [yvWETH](https://etherscan.io/token/0xa258C4606Ca8206D8aA700cE2143D7db854D168c) and start generating yield on this.

## Endpoints

To obtain DeFi positions tokens you can use:
    1. [route](../router-api/api-reference/overview) singular endpoint
    2. [route action](../bundler-api/actions/route) inside of the [bundler endpoint](../bundler-api/api-reference/overview)
    3. [deposit action](../bundler-api/actions/deposit) inside of the [bundler endpoint](../bundler-api/api-reference/overview)

### GET [/api/v1/defiTokens](todo)
This endpoint returns all DeFi position tokens that are possible through Enso.

### GET [/api/v1/metadata](todo)
